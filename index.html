Lean Data

Are you a CIO or CTO? Do you run a small/medium enterprise? Are you a mother of two re-entering the workforce? A retired teacher?

So you have heard about the data revolution, about data being the new oil, about Big Data and NoSQL? And you ask yourself: What's behind it? Why bother? Will it really change the way we work and live?

Search no longer. We, Ina and Michael, will explain you the why and what of what we call lean data. We will show you how the data we humans and also our devices (like your mobile phone) produce can overwhelm us but can also be utilised. It can be used to make better decisions, to find new customers, to optimise a supply chain, to reduce costs in a power plant, to help the environment, to forecast weather, to build trust into your teenage kids or to simply live a happier life.
